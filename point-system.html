Points due to fluctuation

Stock momentum is decided on the basis of its price fluctuation. SPL brings unique pattern to calculate the points based on stock fluctuation. The difference between fluctuation of High price to Open price and fluctuation of Open price to Low price is calculated and the points are awarded for this difference. For Bullish selection, positive difference gives positive points and negative difference gives negative points.

Scenario Stock Open Low High Difference Low % High % Difference Points
