Can I change my email ID?

Complete the following steps to change your email id.

  1. Click on the more option on the Top left of the dashboard.

I forgot my User ID. What should I do?

We suggest you to write an email to support@stockpremierleague.com immediately from registered email ID and by mentioning details like name, mobile number etc. We will inform you the steps to be followed to get it back now.

What to do if OTP is not received?

After entering your mobile number, an OTP (One Time Password) shall be sent to your mobile number within two minutes. Kindly make sure that:

C) How to play (Create / Manage portfolio, Join contests)

How to play?

Please refer our detailed guide on How to play at https://stockpremierleague.com/how-to-play/

  • A User will have option to select mode of the game viz Daily/Weekly/Multibagger
  • A user will select the battle that he is willing to participate in.
  • A user will select an existing (for returning users only) or create new team/portfolio by clicking on the desired Battle from either the “Battles” section or the “My Battle” section under “Upcoming Leagues”.
  • A user has liberty to play for a free battles or by paying battle entry fee. A user then has to create their winning team by choosing variety of stock available in a particular sectors between which a battle is organized.
  • Stocks Selection:
    - For Daily/Weekly Battles: User has to select 12 stocks (Maximum 7 from either sector) to create a team.
    - For Multibagger Battles: User has to select 12/10/8 stocks (Maximum 6/5/4 from the indices) to create a team.
    - A user can create multiple team and can opt to enter with any team that he has created by paying entry fee.
  • The user enters the battle directly (before the entry deadline) if its free, or is prompted to pay the entry fees for the particular contest via various payment options like Debit card, Credit card, Net banking, Mobile wallets etc. to enter.
  • Portfolio value gets changed once the battle is started with respect to change in the selected stocks value.
  • The leader board ranking will be displayed through the Battle period and final winners shall be shown on the top of the leader board once the time of the battle is elapsed.

When do the points get updated?

Points are usually updated every now & then dynamically! However, when the battle ends, the status changes from Live’ to ‘Points in Review’. During this time frame, the final points including your team's total and your ranks are updated. Winners are declared and winnings are distributed only after our team verifies the final points. This process may take up to 1-2 hours. In certain cases, it could take longer. After the winners are declared, the match status will be ‘Completed’

What is point system?

D) Financial Transactions

What if my deposit money is not reflected in my account?

It may be due to an issue at the bank’s gateway. Rest assured, if the amount is debited from your bank account but is not credited in your Stock Premier League account, it will be refunded to you by your bank. It usually takes 5-7 working days for the refund.

Where do I check my Stock Premier League account balance?

You can check your account balance under “Wallet” in “Menu tab”. The account balance is available in 3 categories.

  • Cash Wallet – Amount added by you to your Stock Premier League account which is not yet used to join any cash contest.
  • Winning Wallet – Amount earned by you by joining and winning cash Battles.
  • Affiliate Amount – Amount added to your Stock Premier League account as a freebie which can be used to join more contests.

Where do I check my transaction history on Stock Premier League?

Go to Menu tab -> Wallet -> Transaction.
All transactions done on Stock Premier League will be visible there.

Can I transfer balance from one Stock Premier League account to another?

No, you cannot transfer the balance to any other Stock Premier League account. You can transfer your “winning balance” to your own bank account.

How is my account balance utilised while paying an entry fee?

Account balance utilised in below manner

  1. Bonus if any applicable
  2. Winning Amount
  3. Deposit Balance

How do I add cash to my Stock Premier League accoun?

My withdrawal request is cancelled, what should I do now?

How is the prize money distributed when the contest has multiple winners?

Amount for the multiple winners on the same rank will get distributed by splitting the winning amount equally and the subsequent amount will be skipped.
For example, if there are 3 teams on rank 13, so the next rank will be rank 16. The total winning amount from rank 13, 14 and 15 will be equally split between the rank holders and credited as their winning amount to all 3 winners.

E) Fair Play Violation

Which are the activities not allowed or not appreciated by Stock Premier League Platform?

  • Creating multiple accounts on Stock Premier League.
  • Misusing his/her referral code by creating different fake accounts on Stock Premier League in order to earn Cash Bonus.
  • A user is not allowed to submit fake or some else’s documents.

What happens if a single user creates multiple IDs or accounts or uses Stock premier league multiple accounts in order to earn Cash Bonus?

In case, a user is using a single device for creating multiple accounts on Stock Premier League, it should be ensured by the user that all the mandatory verification formalities including mobile, email, PAN card & Bank Account details verification has been done by the user for all the accounts created. Abiding by the said process will help such users, who create multiple accounts on a single device, in terms of smooth withdrawals on Stock Premier League.

What happens if a user tries to Violate Fair Play Policy of Stock Premier League by converting or trying to convert Cash Bonuses into winning amount?

In an event any user carries out activities that violate the Fair Play Policies of Stock Premier League for converting cash bonus into winnings, Stock Premier League shall block the user refraining him from using the services & Features provided by Stock Premier League alongside resetting the Stock Premier League User Account value to “0” by deducting all the amount present in his/her Stock Premier League User Account.
