INTRODUCTION

Stock Premier League (SPL) is a fun and engaging fantasy stock gaming platform for all Stock market enthusiasts who have a fair understanding of these markets. To become the next ‘Big Bull’, Stock Premier League gives you a chance to test your knowledge and skills, create teams to build or enhance your stock analytical skills, improve your investment methods while winning cash daily.
In Stock Premier League nothing stands in the way of the best winnings! Learn how to play the stock market while simultaneously building, learning, and developing your skills and strategies to win real money.
In Stock Premier League there is three different gameplay available Daily, Multi-Days and Multibagger.

Let's get started with Stock Premier League app. Before understanding how to play with stock we need to register in the app,
follow below steps to register yourself in the app.

Step 1



Enter your phone number and request OTP.

Step 2



Enter OTP.

Step 3



Enter your full name and e-mail id.

Well done! Now let's check below steps to how to select league.

Step 1



Select any game tab from 'Daily/Multi-Day/Multibagger' and for daily or multi-days you can select game type as 'Bullish or Bearish'.

Daily/Multi-day



Here we can check start and end date-time of league, remaining time to start league, Maximum price pool avaiable in league, league type and battle sectors. To enter into league click on card.

Multibagger



Here you can check multibagger league type, start and end date-time, price pool, remaining time and entry fee. Click on entry fee to join battle.

In Daily and Multi-days you can create Bullish or Bearish team as per your choise with your knowledge of stock market.

Step 1



Click on button to enter into league.

Step 2



After entring into league you can see list of battles. Here you can check battle entry fee, battle price pool, available entry slots. click on price to join into battle.

Step 3



After entering battle now you can add stock in your team list by clicking on '+' sign. Nevigate between caps by clicking on tab 'Large, Mid, Small, E-small'.

Step 4



After selecting stock list now select your teams Suprem Stock, Lead Stock and Chief stock by click respective buttons 'SS, LS, CS'.

Step 5



After clicking on 'Team Preview' button you can bt able to see your stock team list. Click on back to go into previous step and click on 'Save Team' to proceed with joining battle.

Step 6



Now pay joining fee of battle and just wait for your winnings.

    Rules for Creating team in daily/multi-days:
    • Every stock team you build on Stock Premier League must have 12 stock.
    • A maximum 7 stocks can be selected from any sector.
    • A minimum 2 stocks should be selected from every cap.
    • Your stock team has to be within the 110 credit cap.

    Your team must qualify the following team selection criteria:

    Stock Cap Min Max
    Large cap 2 4
    Mid cap 2 4
    Small cap 2 4
    Extra Small cap 2 4

  • It’s now time to assign a Supreme Stock, Lead Stock and Chief Stock for your team.
    1. The Supreme Stock will give you 2x points for bullish game and 1/2x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle
    2. The Lead Stock will give you 1.5x points for bullish game and 1/1.5x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle
    3. The Chief Stock will give you 1.25x points for bullish game and 1/1.25x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle

In multibagger there is more power in your hand to create team than daily and multidays. Here you can select stock as bullish or bearish and create team as per your choise with your knowledge of stock market.
Before understanding steps to create multibagger team we need to know leagues and types of multibagger

Types of multibagger:
  • Nifty 30
  • Nifty 50
  • Nifty Next 50
  • Nifty Midcap 50
  • Nifty Smallcap 50
League in multibagger:
  • Top 8 League
  • Top 10 League
  • Top 12 League

Steps to create team in multibagger battle:

Step 1



Click on 'Multibagger' tab to check available battle in multibagger.

Step 2



Here you can check battle entry fee, battle price pool, available entry slots. click on price to join into battle.

Step 3



After entering battle now you can see 2 button in front for every stock 'Bull' & 'Bear'. Select one to add stock in team. Nevigate between groups by clicking on tab 'Group A, Group B, Group C and Group D'.

Step 4



After selecting stock list now select your teams Suprem Stock, Lead Stock and Chief stock by clicking on respective buttons 'SS, LS, CS'.

Step 5



After clicking on 'Team Preview' button you can be able to see you stock team list. Click on back to go into previous step and click on 'Save Team' to proceed with joining battle.

Step 6



Now pay joining fee of battle and just wait for your winnings.

Rules for Creating team in multibagger:
  • Every stock team you build on Stock Premier League must have 8/10/12 stock as per league you are selecting. If you select nifty 30 your league will be Top 8 league so you stock team will be 8 and for other battle your stock team will be 10 or 12 as per your selected league.
  • A maximum 2/3/5 stocks can be selected from any group based on selected league.
  • A minimum 2 stocks should be selected from every group.
  • Your stock team has to be within the credit cap.

Your team must qualify the following team selection criteria:

    Group Min Top 8 Top 10 Top 12
    Group A 2 2 4 5
    Group B 2 2 4 5
    Group C 2 2 4 5
    Group D 2 2 4 5

  • It’s now time to assign a Supreme Stock, Lead Stock and Chief Stock for your team.
    1. The Supreme Stock will give you 2x points for bullish game and 1/2x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle
    2. The Lead Stock will give you 1.5x points for bullish game and 1/1.5x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle
    3. The Chief Stock will give you 1.25x points for bullish game and 1/1.25x points for bearish game scored by them in the actual battle
