Fantasy Point System

Are you just getting started with Fantasy apps? Whether it is fantasy sports or fantasy stocks, having an understanding about the points system is the best way to select matches and get started with it. The total points calculated for each match, will be basis the grid below. So, pick your league and contest, select your stocks and map your scorecard as the contest results are shown. Points diue to fluctuation

Stock momentum is decided on the basis of its price fluctuation. SPL brings unique pattern to calculate the points based on stock fluctuation. The difference between of High price to Close price and Close price to Low price is calculated and the points are awarded for this difference as a percentage. For Bullish selection, positive difference gives positive points and negative difference gives negative points.

Scenario Stock Open Low High Difference Low % High % Difference Points

3 SBI 500 510 520 30 2.00% 4.00% 6.00% 60
4 SBI 500 501 512 13 0.20% 2.40% 2.60% 26
