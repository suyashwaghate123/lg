        • Daily/Weekly: In daily and weekly leagues, user is required to create a team by selecting 12 stocks from each sector (between which league is organised) within the total allocated credits of 100.         • Multibagger: In Multibagger leagues, user is required to create a team by selecting 6/8/10/12 stocks from the NIFTY indices (NIFTY 50, NIFTY30, NIFTYSMALL CAP, BANK NIFTY, NIFTY METAL etc) within the total allocated credits of 60/80/100/120. User can select the stocks from each indices as per league requirement. The user shall also select the Supreme Stock (SS), Lead Stock (LS) and Chief Stock (CS) from the selected 6/8/10/12 stocks in a team which shall enable user to earn 2X, 1.5X and 1.25X points in case of stock scores positive points and 1/2X, 1/1.5X & 1.25X in case of stock scores negative points on selected SS, LS and CS respectively.
