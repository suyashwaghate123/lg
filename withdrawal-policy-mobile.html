Withdrawal Policy

As a onetime process to be eligible for withdrawal, users are required to make a deposit of a minimum of INR 50 at least once in your Stock Premier League account to be eligible for withdrawals.

The minimum and maximum amount for withdrawal in your bank account is INR 100 and INR 250000 once in a day.
Mandatory requirement before first withdrawal in your bank is bank details, personal information, and KYC documents. Once approved, user withdrawal shall be governed within 24-48 working Hours after placement of withdrawal request. User will be eligible to place another withdrawal request after fulfilment of first withdrawal request i.e. after receipt of first withdrawal amount in your bank account. Users are eligible to withdraw winning amount only.

KYC verification includes:
  • PAN Card and address proof.
  • Bank account details should match with the KYC documents uploaded. In case they do not match, we ask for KYC associated with the bank account provided or vice versa.

TDS Rules:
  • A TDS of 31.2% will be deducted at source on net winning amount exceeding INR 10000. The remaining amount will be credited to your Stock Premier League account as Winning amount. Also, a TDS certificate would be duly issued to the user from our end. No TDS will be deducted on withdrawals at all regardless of the amount you wish to withdraw.
  • Once the TDS has been deducted, we will provide the TDS certificate after the end of the following financial quarter.

Paytm Wallet Cash-out Policies
  1. A user will be able transfer winnings directly to your Paytm wallet under the following conditions:
  2. Your Paytm account must be linked to your registered mobile number with Stock Premier League.
  3. The minimum amount you can transfer in your Paytm wallet is Rs 10 & maximum is Rs 5,000 provided your KYC is complete
  4. KYC verified Stock Premier League account and the phone number linked should belong to you.
  5. Your withdrawal request will be processed in maximum 24 hours.
